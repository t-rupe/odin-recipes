Fast and Simple Salsa

Description

This salsa recipe is incredibly simple but still delectable, give it a try and see for yourself!

Ingredients

    6 large tomatoes, chopped
    1 onion, chopped
    3/4 cup green chile peppers, chopped
    1 teaspoon vinegar
    1 teaspoon salt

Steps

    Combine the tomatoes, onion, and green chile peppers in a bowl; drain briefly.
    Return the mixture to the bowl; stir the vinegar and salt into the tomato mixture.
